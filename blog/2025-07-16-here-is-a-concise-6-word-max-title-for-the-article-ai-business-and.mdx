**Judge Nixes Biden Rule, Medical Debt Remains on Credit Reports**

In a recent decision, a federal judge annulled a Biden-era Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) rule that aimed to remove unpaid medical bills from credit reports. This ruling is a setback for those advocating for greater financial transparency and fairness.
