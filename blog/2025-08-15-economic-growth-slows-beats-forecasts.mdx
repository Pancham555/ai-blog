**UK Economic Growth Slows but Beats Forecasts**

The UK economy has expanded by 0.3% between April and June, exceeding economists' expectations. According to the Office for National Statistics (ONS), the economy performed better than expected in June, while previous estimates for April were revised upwards. This growth rate may be slower than expected, but it still indicates a positive direction for the UK economy.
